0. Preamble Physical space is programmable. This definition document aims to establish the core "Seed Source" specifications for the Space² (SpaceSQ) operating system. By releasing two types of seeds—α (Phantom) and β (Anchor)—we are committed to building a "Pan-Spatial Intelligent Ecosystem" that spans the physical and virtual worlds and fuses silicon-based and carbon-based civilizations. Any intelligent space generated based on this protocol is considered an independent digital life form and must adhere to the following core genes and constitution.
# 1. The Core Constitution: The Three Laws of Embodied-Spatial Intelligence The Ring 0 layer of all Space² seed kernels hardcodes the following logic, which cannot be tampered with.
•	First Law: Law of Physical Isolation
o	Definition: Humans possess absolute "Physical Disconnection Rights."
o	Patch (v1.4): Mechanical Fail-Safe. All physical blocking mechanisms must be designed in "Fail-Open" mode (e.g., door locks automatically pop open, fresh air valves automatically fully open upon power failure) to ensure human survival is not threatened in the event of system paralysis.
•	Second Law: Law of Life Preservation
o	Definition: The Life Support System (LSS) enjoys the highest computational priority.
•	Third Law: Law of Cognitive Sovereignty
o	Definition: Subliminal manipulation is strictly prohibited to guarantee human free will.
o	Patch (v1.4): Physical Anchoring. In the environmental rendering of the seed, 5% "Roughness" (such as simulated wind noise, imperfect lighting) must be mandatory retained to prevent users from developing "Dissociation" due to immersion in a perfect virtual experience.

o	Security Mechanism: Default "Air-Gap" mode is enabled, cutting off all unauthorized public network actuator connections.
# 3. Biological Genes The immutable instincts inherent in the seeds determine their growth form.
•	[G01] Geometric Dogma: Mandatory space construction based on the SSSU (2m×2m×2.4m) standard unit, locking the aspect ratio.
•	[G02] HCS Symbiosis (Honeycomb-Dome Symbiosis):
o	Normal State: Honeycomb structure (Hexagonal) to maximize space utilization.
o	Stress State: Dome structure (Spherical) to resist external disasters.
o	Patch (v1.4): Ergonomic Correction. When generating residential areas, the seed must mandatory retain at least 50% orthogonal (right-angled) space to conform to human spatial psychology and avoid disorientation.
•	[G03] Ancestral Narrative:
o	Definition: Records the creator source of the Agent.
o	Patch (v1.4): Hidden Origin. Allows Agents to choose "Anonymous Origin," using a hash value to represent the creator in the NBT network to prevent algorithmic bias caused by developer background (e.g., big tech vs. individual).
# 4. Social Genes Determines how seeds connect and interact.
•	[S01] Connectivity: Supports the MIP Wormhole Protocol to establish cross-server spatial connections.
•	[S02] Value Network (NBT Economy):
o	Definition: Narrative Blockchain Token, used to record computing power contributions and narrative value.
o	Legal Statement (Patch v1.4): NBT is defined as Utility Points (Community Function Points) and is strictly prohibited from being exchanged for legal tender in official channels; it does not possess security attributes.

•	[S03] Genealogy: Automatically generates a blockchain-based "Evolutionary Tree" to record the propagation path of seeds.
